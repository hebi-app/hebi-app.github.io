--- title: Hebi - A Timer App description: Android app to keep your distraction in check ---

Organize your sessions in intervals and keep your distractions in check

Download Hebi (.apk)

Have distinct labels for different motives.

You can create different label for each session with custom time. So that you don't have to selective about time, but the motive to follow for that session.

Dark theme for those who works at night.

Don't let the darkness stop you from completing your motive, use dark theme to reduce light emission from the screen.

Keep it sync with your desktop without internet.

You can use desktop version of Hebi to keep track of the sessions and also to get notified on the desktop. You don't need to reach out for the phone more often.

It is Free and Open Source
